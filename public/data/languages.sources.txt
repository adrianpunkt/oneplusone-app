Language suggestions are limited to official, de facto official, and sufficiently common co-official regional/minority languages.
Known CLDR regional records that are not official languages are excluded from country language suggestions.
English is added client-side as a preferred language everywhere.
Valenciano is added manually for Spain because CLDR represents it under Catalan rather than as a separate language record.
